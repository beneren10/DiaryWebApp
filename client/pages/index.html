Dearest Kitty

Personal : 15/07/1944

I can shake off everything as I write; my sorrows disappear, my courage is reborn. It's really a wonder that I haven't dropped all my ideals, because they seem so absurd and impossible to carry out. Yet I keep them, because in spite of everything, I still believe that people are really good at heart. I don't think of all the misery, but of the beauty that still remains.
